Last Updated: July 22, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Canada Access Hub, a Progressive Web App developed by GiolynxApps ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and complying with applicable laws, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) in Canada and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area (EEA).

Information We Collect

We do not intentionally collect personal data such as names, addresses, email addresses, or phone numbers. However, the app may collect the following non-personal information to enhance functionality and user experience:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address (anonymized where possible).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, session duration, and interaction data (e.g., button clicks), collected anonymously.
  • Local Storage Data: Cached data for offline access via service workers, stored locally on your device.

We do not use cookies that transmit data externally, but local storage may be used for app performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app's functionality and user experience.
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize performance (e.g., fixing bugs, enhancing features).
  • To ensure compliance with legal obligations and protect against fraud or security threats.

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share non-personal, aggregated data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party analytics tools (e.g., no external analytics currently used) to process usage data. These providers are contractually obligated to use the data only for our specified purposes and to maintain confidentiality.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

We do not engage in cross-app tracking or share data with ad networks.

User Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request details of any data we hold about you (if any).
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of data, subject to legal retention periods.
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics collection if supported by the app (e.g., via settings, if implemented).

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. Note that since we primarily collect non-personal data, these rights may have limited applicability.
